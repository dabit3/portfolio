Javascript Animations with Greensock | Introduction

What is Greensock?

- commonly referred to as GSAP (Greensock Animation Plugin)

- simple & intuitive

- cross-browser compatible

- no vendor prefixes needed

- CSS3 transitions & animations don't work in IE9; GSAP does

- animate single object, arrays, or selector objects

- pause(), play(), repeat, yoyo, restart(), seek(), reverse() entire sequences on the fly
